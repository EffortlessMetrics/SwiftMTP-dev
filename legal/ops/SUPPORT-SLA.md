# Support & Maintenance SLA

**Scope:** SwiftMTP core, transport, CLI.

## Tiers

### Standard
- **Hours:** Business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST, Monday-Friday)
- **Response Time:** 2 business days
- **Fixes:** Best-effort fixes in next release
- **Support Channels:** Email, GitHub Issues

### Priority
- **Hours:** 12x5 (8 AM - 8 PM EST, Monday-Friday)
- **Response Time:** 8 hours
- **Fixes:** Security fixes and critical regressions backported to N-1
- **Support Channels:** Email, Slack, Phone

### Enterprise
- **Hours:** 24x7
- **Response Time:** 2 hours for P1 issues
- **Fixes:** P1 hotfixes, advisory help for App Store submissions
- **Support Channels:** Dedicated Slack, Phone, Video

## Severities

- **P1 (Critical):** Production outage, data loss, security breach
- **P2 (Major):** Core function degraded, major feature broken
- **P3 (Minor):** Non-critical function affected, workaround available
- **P4 (Info):** Questions, feature requests, general guidance

## Customer Duties

- Provide reliable reproduction steps
- Include device matrix details (VID:PID, OS version, SwiftMTP version)
- Supply logs generated by `swiftmtp probe` and `swiftmtp bench`
- Test with latest patch version before escalating

## Maintenance Windows

- Scheduled maintenance: Second Tuesday of each month, 2 AM - 4 AM EST
- Emergency maintenance: With 24-hour notice for Enterprise customers

## Exclusions

- Custom modifications and integrations
- Third-party hardware or software issues
- Issues resolved by following documentation
- Beta/pre-release versions

## Service Credits

For SLA breaches:
- P1: 10% credit per hour over SLA
- P2: 5% credit per business day over SLA
- Maximum credit: 100% of monthly fee
